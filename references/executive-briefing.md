> *Cover the following:*
>
> *1. **AWS Usage** — What workloads are on AWS, key services in use, migration status, adoption maturity*
> *2. **PPA / Commercial** — Current commitment status, renewal timeline, spend trajectory*
> *3. **Recent Wins / Momentum** — Positive developments in the past 3–6 months (e.g., completed POC, new workload onboarded, expanded to new BU, successful migration milestone). Helps leadership reinforce positive momentum during the meeting.*
> *4. **Issues / Concerns** — Active escalations, service incidents, unresolved complaints, relationship risks*
